Marcus Draney

Head Coach & Program Director

Marcus Draney is the founder and head coach of West Side Basketball. Based in West Jordan, Utah, Marcus brings a deep passion for the game and a commitment to developing young athletes both on and off the court.

With experience coaching at the youth and AAU levels, Marcus focuses on building strong fundamentals, basketball IQ, and the competitive mindset players need to succeed at the next level.
